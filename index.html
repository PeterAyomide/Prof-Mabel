Biography

Prof. Mabel Omowumi Ipinmoroti is a Professor of Fisheries and Aquaculture with over two decades of academic, research, and administrative experience. She holds a PhD, MSc, and BSc in Fisheries Management from the University of Ibadan.

Academic Qualifications

  • PhD – Fisheries Management, University of Ibadan (1997–2005)
  • MSc – Fisheries Management, University of Ibadan (1992–1994)
  • BSc – Fisheries Management, University of Ibadan (1986–1991)

Research & Expertise

  • Sustainable Aquaculture Systems
  • Fish Nutrition & Alternative Protein Sources
  • Environmental Toxicology
  • Ecological Organic Agriculture (EOA)

Selected Publications

  • Utilization of Housefly Maggots as Replacement for Fish Meal
  • Assessment of Microplastics in Nigerian Inland Waters
  • Diversity and Distribution of Fish Species in Lake Asejire
