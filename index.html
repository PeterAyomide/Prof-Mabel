01 / Overview

Research Expertise

My research addresses critical challenges in sustainable aquaculture and fisheries management in Nigeria and West Africa, with a focus on environmental toxicology, alternative protein sources, and ecological approaches to fish production.

Sustainable Aquaculture

  • Alternative protein sources (insect-based feeds)
  • Organic fish production systems
  • Feed formulation and nutrition
  • Growth performance optimization

Environmental Toxicology

  • Heavy metal contamination in aquatic systems
  • Microplastics assessment in fish species
  • Pesticide toxicity studies
  • Histopathological biomarkers

Fisheries Ecology & Management

  • Fish diversity and distribution in Nigerian waters
  • Climate change impacts on fisheries
  • Recreational fisheries development
  • Artisanal fisheries socioeconomics

Ecological Organic Agriculture

  • EOA implementation in aquaculture
  • Natural preservatives for fish products
  • Organic feed production
  • Sustainable farming practices
