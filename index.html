Biography

Prof. Mabel Omowumi Ipinmoroti is a distinguished Nigerian academic with over two decades of experience in fisheries, aquaculture, environmental management, and organic agriculture. She holds a Ph.D. in Fisheries Management from the University of Ibadan and is currently a Professor at Osun State University.
