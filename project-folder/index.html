Biography

Mabel Omowumi Ipinmoroti is a distinguished Professor of Fisheries and Aquaculture at Osun State University, Nigeria. With a Ph.D. in Fisheries Management from the University of Ibadan, she has dedicated over two decades to researching sustainable aquaculture, environmental management, and organic agriculture.


She currently leads groundbreaking research on the nutrition and health implications of processed insects as protein supplements in fish diets, supported by the TETFund National Research Fund. She acts as a Team Leader for the Ecological Organic Agriculture (EOA) initiative in Nigeria.
